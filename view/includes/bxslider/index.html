The Department of Ecumenical Relations and Development (DERD) is a nonprofit organization belonging to the Greek Orthodox Patriarchate of Antioch and All the East. DERD operates from the Patriarchate located in the old city of Damascus. In 1986, the Patriarchate formed a department to strongly focus on fundraising and development activities to finance several Patriarchal community projects.
